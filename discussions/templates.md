### SMART Goals Template

```markdown
# SMART Goal Setting for DSA Learning

## Goal Template
**Specific:** [What exactly do you want to achieve?]
**Measurable:** [How will you measure progress?]
**Achievable:** [Is this realistic given your constraints?]
**Relevant:** [How does this align with your career goals?]
**Time-bound:** [When will you complete this?]

## Example SMART Goals

### Short-term Goals (1-2 weeks)
1. **Goal:** Master linked list operations
   - **Specific:** Understand and implement singly, doubly, and circular linked lists
   - **Measurable:** Complete all 15 linked list problems in the course
   - **Achievable:** Dedicate 2 hours daily to linked lists
   - **Relevant:** Foundation for tree and graph algorithms
   - **Time-bound:** Complete by [Date, 2 weeks from now]

### Medium-term Goals (1 month)
2. **Goal:** Become proficient in tree algorithms
   - **Specific:** Master binary trees, BST, and basic tree traversals
   - **Measurable:** Solve 50 tree-related problems on LeetCode
   - **Achievable:** Build on linked list knowledge
   - **Relevant:** Essential for technical interviews
   - **Time-bound:** Complete by [Date, 1 month from now]

### Long-term Goals (3 months)
3. **Goal:** Be interview-ready for mid-level positions
   - **Specific:** Master all fundamental DSA concepts and solve 300+ problems
   - **Measurable:** Pass mock interviews with 80%+ success rate
   - **Achievable:** Following structured learning plan
   - **Relevant:** Career advancement to senior developer role
   - **Time-bound:** Complete by [Date, 3 months from now]
```
